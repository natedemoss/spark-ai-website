Spark AI is an Electron app built using HTML and JavaScript, providing a lightweight and transparent chatbot widget for your desktop.

Commands:

Ctrl + Shift + X: Hide widget

Ctrl + R: Clear messages

Note: These commands may not work consistently in all scenarios due to system or app focus issues.
